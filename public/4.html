YOUR INNER VOICE

To realize a face to face encounter with your inner voice we would like you to write a message to yourself.

What would you like to say to yourself?

Please write below (in english) what you would like to say to yourself more often. Take a moment to decide what you would like to say and what needs to be said.

We appreciate the intimacy of this question, do not feel rushed or pressured. Your contribution will become part of an anonymous chorus of voices that will be accessible online.

Thank you for your contribution
