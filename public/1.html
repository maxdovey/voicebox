“Take the words right out of my mouth” invites you to converse with your ‘internal monologue' through speech synthesis and lets you hear your inner voice out loud.

In a world dominated by text based conversation we want to explore the chasm between our internal (silent) voice in contrast to our spoken voice.

What happens when the internal voice becomes an embodied object?

Using machine learning to replicate your voice
we invite you to take the time to contribute and listen to your internal voice spoken aloud.
