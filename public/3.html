To generate your inner voice we first need an audio fingerprint of what your spoken voice sounds like. The system will then be able to style your inner voice to sound similar to your spoken voice. Unfortunately, the system is only able to recognise the English language.

YOUR VOICE VOW

To take a fingerprint of your voice you will need to say the sentence in bold below out loud into the microphone, including your first name. Make sure the recording is audible, speak slowly and clearly and if you make a mistake you can start again. When you are ready press record to begin and stop when you have finished the recording.

“I, (insert name) would like to hear my internal voice out loud”
